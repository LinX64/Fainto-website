FAQ

Fainto — frequently asked questions

Is Fainto free?

Yes. Fainto is free to use, and the core budget tracker and free-tier AI coach cost nothing. A Premium tier unlocks larger on-device AI models and optional Cloud AI, but you can track your money and get coaching without paying.

Does Fainto need an account?

No account is required. You can open Fainto and start tracking immediately. Accounts are optional, so you never have to hand over an email to see your own money.

Does Fainto work offline?

Yes. The AI models and your data live on the phone, so Fainto works fully offline — you can log spending and get coaching with the network off. The only feature that needs a connection is opt-in Cloud AI, which is off unless you turn it on.

What data does Fainto collect?

By default, nothing about your money leaves the device. The website has no cookies, analytics, or trackers, and the app is built so that your financial data stays on your phone. The app can show you 0 requests and 0 bytes sent. The only time anything is sent is when you deliberately opt in to Cloud AI.

Is my financial data uploaded to the cloud?

Not by default. Fainto is private by architecture, not by policy: your data has nowhere to go unless you choose it. Cloud AI via OpenRouter is the only feature that sends anything out, and it is strictly opt-in — nothing routes to it until you enable it yourself.

What AI models run on-device in Fainto?

Five language models run locally on your phone. On the free tier: SmolLM 135M and Qwen 2.5 0.5B. On Premium: Qwen 2.5 1.5B, DeepSeek R1 1.5B, and Phi-4 Mini 3.8B. All five run on-device, so the coaching happens on your phone rather than on a server.

What is Cloud AI and is it optional?

Cloud AI is an optional Premium feature that routes your questions to a larger model through OpenRouter when your phone can't hold one big enough. It is strictly opt-in and off by default — if you never enable it, nothing about your money is ever sent to the cloud.

Which countries does the tax engine support?

Fainto's built-in income-tax engine covers 45 countries — 44 country configurations plus a bespoke Poland engine — so the numbers reflect where you actually live rather than a generic default.

How many languages does Fainto support?

The interface is localised in 25 languages, so the app reads naturally in your own language and pairs with the multi-country tax engine for your region.

Is there an iPhone or iOS version of Fainto?

Not yet. Fainto is available on Android now, and an iOS version is coming soon. There is no iPhone download link at this time.

Does Fainto show ads?

No. There are no ads in Fainto — nothing is wedged between your transactions — and there are no trackers, analytics, or cookies watching what you do.

Where can I download Fainto?

Fainto is on Google Play, listed as "Fainto: AI Finance Coach", at https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.vaultai.app. It is an Android app; iOS is coming soon.

Is Fainto financial advice?

No. Fainto provides educational information to help you understand and plan your money, including tax estimates. It is not financial or tax advice.

How does the on-device AI coach work?

One of five local language models reads your spending and answers your money questions directly on the phone. You can pick a lightweight model for speed or a larger one for more depth, and it all runs without a round trip to the cloud.

Is Fainto really private if it uses AI?

Yes. The AI runs on-device, so using the coach does not send your data anywhere. Because the models are local, private AI and privacy are not a trade-off in Fainto — the coaching happens on your phone by default.

What does "private by architecture, not by policy" mean?

It means the privacy guarantee is built into how the app works, not just promised in a document. Your money data stays on the device because it has nowhere else to go by default, rather than because a policy says a company won't misuse it.
