Privacy

Privacy Policy

Fainto is built so your money has no reason to leave your phone. This website follows the same rule: it collects nothing about you. This policy explains exactly what that means, including under the EU/UK GDPR.

What this website collects

Nothing. There are no cookies, no analytics, no advertising or tracking scripts, and no fingerprinting. The fonts are served from this domain — not Google — so opening the site makes no third-party requests. There is no account, login, or newsletter here.

Server logs

Our web host may keep standard, short-lived server access logs (which can include your IP address and browser type) purely to keep the site secure and online. We do not read, sell, or use them to identify or profile you.

The contact form

The form on our Contact page never sends anything to a server. When you press Send, your own email app opens with the message pre-filled and you decide whether to send it to support@fainto.app. If you do email us, we use your message only to reply.

The Fainto app

The Android app is offline-first: your financial data stays on your device and nothing is uploaded by default. Optional Cloud AI is opt-in only. The app requires no account. See the in-app privacy details for specifics.

Cookies & consent

This site sets no cookies and runs no tracking, so under the GDPR and the ePrivacy (“cookie law”) rules there is nothing that requires a consent banner — which is why you won't see one.

Your GDPR rights

If we ever hold personal data of yours — for example, an email you chose to send us — you have the right to access it, correct it, ask us to delete it, or object to its use. Email support@fainto.app and we'll action your request. You also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

Data controller

Fainto (Mo) is the data controller. The best way to reach us about privacy is support@fainto.app.

Changes to this policy

If anything changes, we'll update this page and the date below.
