Privacy

Privacy Policy

This policy covers both the Fainto Android app and this website. In short: the financial data you enter stays on your device, we run no ads or trackers, and nothing about your money is uploaded unless you deliberately switch on an optional feature. It's written to meet the EU/UK GDPR and Google Play's User Data policy.

Who we are

Fainto is made by Mo. For anything privacy-related — including exercising your rights below — contact support@fainto.app. This is also the data controller under the GDPR.

What the app stores, and where

Everything you put into Fainto — accounts, balances, transactions, income, bills, budgets and the figures used by the tax tools — is stored locally on your device. There is no Fainto account and no sign-up, so we never receive, see, or store your financial data on any server. It is not collected by us, and never sold, rented, or shared.

The on-device AI coach

Fainto's AI coach runs language models directly on your phone. In the default on-device mode, your questions and financial context are processed locally and never leave the device.

Optional Cloud AI (off by default)

Fainto includes an optional Cloud AI mode — a Premium feature that is turned off unless you enable it. If, and only if, you switch it on and send a message, the text of that message is transmitted over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to our AI gateway, OpenRouter, and the model provider you choose, solely to generate a reply. We don't use it for advertising. You can leave Cloud AI off to stay fully on-device; if you use it, please also review OpenRouter's own privacy terms.

What we never do

No advertising, no analytics or usage tracking, no third-party trackers or profiling SDKs, and no selling or sharing of your personal or financial data. By default the app makes 0 requests and sends 0 bytes — by design.

Permissions

The app requests only what a feature needs — chiefly internet access, used to download the on-device AI models you choose and to power optional Cloud AI. Granting or withholding access is your choice.

Keeping and deleting your data

Because your data lives on your device, you control it. To remove everything, clear the app's data or uninstall Fainto — there is no server-side account to delete. If you have emailed us, you can ask us to delete that correspondence at any time.

Security

Keeping your data on your device rather than on our servers removes the biggest source of risk. Any optional cloud request is sent over encrypted HTTPS.

Children

Fainto is a general-audience finance tool and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent age where you live). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

This website

fainto.app sets no cookies and runs no analytics, ads, or trackers; the fonts are served from this domain, so opening the site makes no third-party requests. The contact form sends nothing to a server — pressing Send opens your own email app so you decide whether to email us. With no cookies or tracking, no consent banner is required. Our web host may keep standard short-lived access logs (which can include your IP) purely to keep the site online.

Your GDPR rights

If we ever hold personal data of yours (for example, an email you send us), you have the right to access, correct, delete, or object to its use, and to complain to your local data-protection authority. Email support@fainto.app and we'll action it.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the date below.
