About

I just wanted a money app that was on my side.

Every finance app I tried was quietly working against me — ads wedged between my own transactions, my spending sold on to “partners,” something phoning home with every tap.

So I built Fainto. It runs entirely on your phone, shows no ads, and by default sends nothing about your money anywhere. Real language models do the coaching on-device; a built-in tax engine covers 45 countries in 25 languages. Free to start, no account, no tracking.

It’s the app I wanted to exist: one that gives you a clear view of your money — and then keeps it to itself.

— Mo, maker of Fainto
