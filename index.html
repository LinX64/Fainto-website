What it is

An AI money coach that runs on your phone, not on our servers.

  • The AI is on-device

    Five language models run locally — from a 135M featherweight up to Phi-4 Mini 3.8B — so the coaching happens on your phone, not on a server.

  • Nothing uploaded by default

    Private by architecture, not policy. The app can show 0 requests, 0 bytes — by default, by design. Cloud AI is opt-in only.

    A built-in engine runs real income-tax rules for 45 countries, with the app localised in 25 languages — so the numbers fit where you actually live.
