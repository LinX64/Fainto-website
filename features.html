Features

What Fainto does

Fainto is a private, offline-first budget tracker with an on-device AI money coach. Here is everything it does — and if you want the story behind it, read why Fainto was built.

An AI money coach that runs on your phone

The coaching in Fainto is done by real language models that run on your phone, not on a server. Five models ship on-device: SmolLM 135M and Qwen 2.5 0.5B on the free tier, and Qwen 2.5 1.5B, DeepSeek R1 1.5B, and Phi-4 Mini 3.8B on Premium. Pick the featherweight for speed or the larger models for depth — they read your spending and answer your questions without a round trip to the cloud. If you want a bigger model than your phone can hold, Cloud AI via OpenRouter is available on Premium, but it is strictly opt-in: nothing routes to it unless you turn it on yourself.

Private by architecture, not by policy

Most apps promise privacy in a policy you have to trust. Fainto is built so the data simply has nowhere to go: by default, nothing about your money leaves the device. The app can show you 0 requests, 0 bytes — by default, by design. Cloud AI is the only feature that ever sends anything out, and it is off until you opt in. That is what “private by architecture, not by policy” means — the guarantee is in how the app is built, not in a paragraph of legal text. Review how Fainto handles your data for the full detail.

Works fully offline

Because the models and your data live on the phone, Fainto works with the network off. Log spending on a plane, in a tunnel, or with data disabled — the AI coach still answers and the tax math still runs. There is no server to be down and no sync to wait on. The one exception is opt-in Cloud AI, which naturally needs a connection when you choose to use it.

Free, no account, no ads

Fainto is free to use and needs no account — open it and start tracking. Accounts are optional, so nothing forces you to hand over an email to see your own money. There are no ads wedged between your transactions and no trackers, analytics, or cookies watching what you do. Premium exists for the larger AI models and Cloud AI, but the core budget tracker and the free-tier coach cost nothing.

A built-in tax engine for 45 countries

Fainto includes an income-tax engine that runs real rules for 45 countries — 44 country configurations plus a bespoke Poland engine — so the numbers reflect where you actually live rather than a generic US default. Set a gross figure and see the tax come out: in the Poland preset, for example, PLN 240,000 in income resolves to PLN 149,787 after the country’s rules. The estimates are educational information to help you plan, not financial or tax advice.

Localised in 25 languages

The interface is translated into 25 languages, so the app reads naturally whether you budget in English, Polish, German, French, or another supported locale. Localisation pairs with the multi-country tax engine — currency, tax rules, and wording line up with your region instead of forcing you to translate a US-only app in your head.

See it in the app

The Overview brings it together: net income 12 482,22 zł a month (up 56.7%), split into income 12.5K, expenses 1.0K, and savings 7.1K zł, above AI insights, your accounts, and upcoming bills. The net-worth view totals your assets — 107 400,00 zł in the sample — against your liabilities. Every figure here is on-device and yours alone. Available on Android now — get Fainto on Google Play — and iOS is coming soon.
